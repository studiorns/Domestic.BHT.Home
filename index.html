Abu Dhabi Domestic Brand Health Reports

Welcome to the Department of Culture and Tourism - Abu Dhabi's Domestic Brand Health reporting portal. These reports provide comprehensive insights and strategic recommendations based on three key research studies conducted in 2024-2025:

  • UAE Brand Tracker (Q4'24) - Quarterly survey of 800 UAE residents outside Abu Dhabi
  • Destination Visitor Survey (DVS) 2024 - Face-to-face interviews with 2,500 domestic visitors
  • Nightlife Survey 2024 - Online survey of 1,500 UAE residents focused on nightlife preferences

A concise overview of key findings from all three research studies, highlighting Abu Dhabi's domestic brand health metrics, visitor insights, and nightlife trends.
